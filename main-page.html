Frequently Asked Questions

How soon will a provider be able to meet with me?

Our current wait times are 3-4 weeks. In the case of an emergency, please call 911.

How much will I pay out of pocket?

Our services are sliding scale based. On intake, you will complete a form to help us better understand your financial situation.

Do you offer telehealth visits?

Each patient will work with their provider to create a plan for their meetings. If telehealth is what is comfortable, we certainly can make it work!
